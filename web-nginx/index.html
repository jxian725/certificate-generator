Individual Certificate Retrieval Guide

Notes: This guide explains how participants can retrieve and download their individual certificates (Certificate of Participation or Award Certificate, etc.) from the Huawei Developer Competition system.

1. Overview

Participants can access their certificates online via the Huawei Developer Competition Certificate System. The system verifies participant identity based on a combination of specific details and only generates the certificate if the provided information matches the records.

2. Required Information

To retrieve your certificate, you will need to provide one of the following valid combinations:
i. Country + Full Name
ii. Country + Email Address
iii. Country + Huawei Cloud Account ID

Notes:
• Ensure your entries match exactly with the information registered during the competition. (refer to the screenshots below)
• The system is case-insensitive but may require spaces and special characters to match exactly.

3. Important Notes

• Only participants who registered on the official Huawei Developer Competition (Asia Pacific or Northern Africa regions) website can retrieve certificates.
• If you encounter any issues (e.g., cannot retrieve certificate, missing record), contact the competition committee team: hwcdeveloper@huawei.com (Asia Pacific) or developers.na@huawei.com (Northern Africa)
• Certificates are only available online through this portal — there is no offline distribution.
